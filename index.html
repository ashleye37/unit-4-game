Welcome to Crystal Collector!

Instructions:

  1. You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

  2. There are 4 crystals shown below. Each crystal click will add an different amount of points to your total score.

  3. Win the game by matching your score to the random number. You lose if your score goes over the random number.

  4. The value of each crystal is hidden until clicked and will change value each time the game restarts.
